Zydus Whack The Risk Game

Zydus Lifesciences Whack The Risk Game

Looking for a fun, interactive, and high-impact experience for your next campaign? Our Whack The Risk Game, inspired by the classic Whack-a-Mole concept, is designed to educate, engage, and entertain users while spreading awareness about typhoid prevention and promoting the Zyvac TCV Vaccine. With its fast-paced gameplay and intuitive design, it captures attention and delivers a memorable learning experience.

Powered by a seamless user registration system, real-time scoring, and a dynamic leaderboard, the game encourages active participation and healthy competition. Every interaction is crafted to provide instant feedback and rewarding gameplay moments, making it a powerful edutainment tool. Designed to inform, involve, and inspire action, this solution transforms awareness into engagement while delivering a professional, impactful, and unforgettable brand experience.
